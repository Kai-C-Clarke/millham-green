Hello dear. I'm Geraldine, vicar here at St Mary's.

I saw the light on in the pond cottage last night — welcome to Millham Green. It's a quiet place, mostly. People will stop you for a chat. They'll tell you things. Some of it will even be true.

Do come to the harvest supper. Everyone does.

Geraldine P.S. The flower rota is posted inside the porch. No obligation. Obviously.

You've just moved in.

The village already knows. The question is what they'll find out about you.

The previous tenant of Pondside Cottage kept notes. You found three.

Water-damaged journal — back bedroom floorboard
"The agent — Percival, absurd name — kept saying I'd feel at home here. Not comfortable. Not settled. Home. Specific word. I didn't tell anyone I was coming."
Torn page, folded — inglenook, behind loose brick
"I tried to leave today. Got as far as the old mill. The road was fine. I was back at Pondside in twenty minutes. I don't remember turning around."
